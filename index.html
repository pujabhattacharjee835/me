+

Yes, I offer flexible pricing options depending on your needs. You can choose between a fixed project-based quote or an hourly rate. This gives you full control over your budget and scope.

+

I specialize in UI/UX design, web and app development, frontend/backend coding, landing pages, dashboards, and full-stack solutions. Whether you're starting from scratch or revamping an existing product, I've got you covered.

For design, I use Figma, Adobe XD, and Illustrator. On the development side, I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, and more. I tailor the tech stack based on your project's needs.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. I offer ongoing maintenance, updates, and support packages. Whether it’s fixing bugs, making improvements, or scaling features — I’ve got you covered even after delivery.
